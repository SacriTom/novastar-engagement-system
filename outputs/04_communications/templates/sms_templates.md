## SMS Guidelines

- All messages must be under 160 characters (single SMS segment).
- All messages include a required opt-out instruction.
- SMS is reserved for high-value moments only -- maximum 2 SMS per guest per month.
- Opt-out line ("Reply STOP to opt out") counts toward the 160-character limit.
