## Trigger-Based Push Notifications

All notifications require the guest to have the NovaStar app installed and push permissions enabled. Frequency cap: maximum 3 push notifications per guest per week.

---

### 1. Post-Checkout Recap

| Field | Content |
|-------|---------|
| **Trigger** | Guest completes checkout |
| **Timing** | 2 hours after checkout |
| **Title** | "Your [City] stay recap is ready" |
| **Body** | Highlights, photos, and points earned -- all in one place. Tap to see. |
| **CTA** | Opens Remember Your Stay screen |

### 2. Nostalgia Nudge

| Field | Content |
|-------|---------|
| **Trigger** | 5 days post-checkout |
| **Timing** | 10:00 AM guest local time |
| **Title** | "Missing [City] yet?" |
| **Body** | Your stay memories are saved. Add your photos before they fade. |
| **CTA** | Opens photo upload in travel journal |

### 3. Points Balance Reminder

| Field | Content |
|-------|---------|
| **Trigger** | 14 days post-checkout (loyalty members only) |
| **Timing** | 11:00 AM guest local time |
| **Title** | "You have [X] NovaStar points" |
| **Body** | That is [Y reward] away from [next redemption]. Keep going. |
| **CTA** | Opens Rewards Hub |

### 4. Tier Upgrade

| Field | Content |
|-------|---------|
| **Trigger** | Guest qualifies for new loyalty tier |
| **Timing** | Immediately |
| **Title** | "You just reached [Tier Name]!" |
| **Body** | New perks unlocked: [top benefit]. Tap to see everything you have earned. |
| **CTA** | Opens tier dashboard |

### 5. Personalised Recommendation

| Field | Content |
|-------|---------|
| **Trigger** | 21 days post-checkout; AI recommendation generated |
| **Timing** | 6:00 PM guest local time (evening browsing window) |
| **Title** | "Your next stay idea is ready" |
| **Body** | Based on your [City] trip: [Property Name] from $[price]/night. |
| **CTA** | Opens Your Next Stay recommendation card |

### 6. Price Drop Alert

| Field | Content |
|-------|---------|
| **Trigger** | Rate drops 10%+ for a property or city the guest has viewed or stayed at |
| **Timing** | Within 1 hour of rate change |
| **Title** | "Price drop: [City] from $[new price]/night" |
| **Body** | Rates just fell at NovaStar [Property]. Book direct for the best deal. |
| **CTA** | Opens booking page with pre-filled dates |

### 7. Win-Back Urgency

| Field | Content |
|-------|---------|
| **Trigger** | 30 days post-checkout, no rebooking, guest opened win-back email but did not convert |
| **Timing** | 12:00 PM guest local time |
| **Title** | "[First Name], your personal offer expires soon" |
| **Body** | 3 days left to claim your [offer summary]. Tap to book. |
| **CTA** | Opens direct booking page with offer applied |

### 8. Local Explorer Update

| Field | Content |
|-------|---------|
| **Trigger** | New seasonal content published for a city the guest has stayed in |
| **Timing** | Saturday 9:00 AM guest local time |
| **Title** | "New in [City]: [experience headline]" |
| **Body** | [One-line teaser, e.g., "A rooftop wine bar just opened near your old hotel."] |
| **CTA** | Opens Local Explorer for that city |

### 9. Milestone Celebration

| Field | Content |
|-------|---------|
| **Trigger** | Guest completes 5th, 10th, or 20th night |
| **Timing** | Immediately at checkout |
| **Title** | "[X] nights with NovaStar -- thank you!" |
| **Body** | We added [bonus reward] to your account to celebrate. |
| **CTA** | Opens Rewards Hub with milestone animation |

### 10. Birthday Greeting

| Field | Content |
|-------|---------|
| **Trigger** | Guest's birthday (if date of birth captured) |
| **Timing** | 8:00 AM guest local time |
| **Title** | "Happy birthday, [First Name]!" |
| **Body** | A little something from us: [birthday reward, e.g., 500 bonus points]. Enjoy your day. |
| **CTA** | Opens Rewards Hub |
