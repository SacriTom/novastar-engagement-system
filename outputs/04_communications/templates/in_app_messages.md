## Contextual In-App Messages

These messages appear as modal overlays, bottom sheets, or inline cards within the NovaStar app based on the guest's context and behaviour. Frequency cap: maximum 1 in-app message per session.

---

### 1. Welcome to Rewards (First App Open After Sign-Up)

| Field | Content |
|-------|---------|
| **Trigger** | Guest opens app for the first time after enrolling in NovaStar Rewards |
| **Headline** | "Welcome to NovaStar Rewards, [First Name]" |
| **Body** | You are an Explorer. Complete your profile to earn 500 bonus points -- that is halfway to a free late checkout. |
| **CTA Button** | "Complete My Profile" |
| **Format** | Full-screen modal with tier badge graphic |

### 2. Post-Stay Recap Available

| Field | Content |
|-------|---------|
| **Trigger** | Guest opens app within 7 days of checkout |
| **Headline** | "Your [City] recap is ready" |
| **Body** | We put together a highlight reel of your stay. Take a look -- and add your own photos if you like. |
| **CTA Button** | "View My Stay" |
| **Format** | Bottom sheet with property hero image |

### 3. Tier Progress Nudge

| Field | Content |
|-------|---------|
| **Trigger** | Guest views Rewards Hub and is within 2 stays/5 nights of next tier |
| **Headline** | "You're close to [Next Tier]" |
| **Body** | Just [X more nights] to unlock [top benefit of next tier]. Your next stay gets you there. |
| **CTA Button** | "See What's Next" |
| **Format** | Inline card with progress bar |

### 4. Book Direct Prompt

| Field | Content |
