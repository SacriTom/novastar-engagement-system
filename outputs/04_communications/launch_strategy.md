# NovaStar Post-Stay Engagement System -- Launch Strategy

**Prepared by:** Casey the Communicator (Agent 04, Team AWESOME)
**Date:** 10 February 2026

---

## 1. Three-Phase Rollout Overview

| Phase | Timeframe | Objective |
|-------|-----------|-----------|
| **Teaser** | Weeks -2 to -1 (pre-launch) | Build internal readiness and guest curiosity |
| **Launch** | Week 1 | Activate all channels; enrol first wave of loyalty members |
| **Sustain** | Months 1-3 | Optimise, expand segments, prove ROI |

---

## 2. Phase 1 -- Teaser (2 Weeks Pre-Launch)

### External (Guest-Facing)
- **Week -2:** "Something new is coming to NovaStar" social media countdown tiles (Instagram, Facebook, LinkedIn).
- **Week -1:** Teaser email to all existing contacts: "We remembered everything about your last stay. Ready to see?" Preview of the Remember Your Stay feature.
- **Week -1:** In-room tent cards at all 45 properties: "Big changes are on the way. Ask the front desk."

### Internal (Staff-Facing)
- **Week -2:** All-hands video briefing from VP of Guest Experience (10 min) covering the "why" -- the 12.1% rebooking gap, the $34.2M revenue opportunity, the competitive score of 9/50.
- **Week -2:** Digital training module deployed to all front-desk, concierge, and GM teams via the internal LMS. Content: new data-capture workflow, loyalty pitch script, FAQ sheet.
- **Week -1:** Property-level "launch huddle" led by each GM. Distribute quick-reference cards for the front-desk checkout script (see on_property_playbook.md).
- **Week -1:** Regional champion nominated per region (Americas, Europe, APAC) to field questions and track adoption.

### Milestones
- [ ] Training module completed by 90%+ of front-line staff.
- [ ] Teaser email sent; baseline open rate recorded.
- [ ] Social countdown assets live across all brand channels.

### Success Criteria
| Metric | Target |
|--------|--------|
| Staff training completion | >= 90% |
| Teaser email open rate | >= 25% |
| Social impressions (teaser posts) | >= 200K |

---

## 3. Phase 2 -- Launch (Week 1)

### External
- **Day 1:** Launch email to full guest database: "NovaStar Rewards just got personal." Introduces new tier system (Explorer/Adventurer/Voyager/Ambassador) and Remember Your Stay.
- **Day 1:** App update goes live with Rewards Hub, Remember Your Stay, and Your Next Stay recommendation cards.
- **Day 2-3:** Push notifications to existing app users: "Your new NovaStar Rewards tier is waiting. Tap to see."
- **Day 3:** Social media launch posts (carousel of features, 30-sec video, guest testimonial mockup).
- **Day 5:** SMS to high-value past guests (Business Regulars, Extended-Stay segments): personalised invite with 500 bonus sign-up points.
- **Day 7:** First Remember Your Stay recap emails sent to guests who checked out during launch week.

### Internal
- **Day 1:** "Go Live" confirmation cascade -- regional champions confirm systems are active at all properties.
- **Day 1-3:** Front-desk teams begin new checkout script; GM spot-checks during peak checkout hours.
- **Day 5:** First adoption report pulled -- data-capture rate, loyalty sign-ups, app downloads.
- **Day 7:** Debrief call with regional champions; identify friction points.

### Milestones
- [ ] Launch email delivered to 100% of contactable guests.
- [ ] App update live on iOS and Android.
- [ ] First 1,000 new loyalty sign-ups recorded.

### Success Criteria
| Metric | Target |
|--------|--------|
| Launch email open rate | >= 30% |
| New loyalty enrolments (Week 1) | >= 1,000 |
| App downloads (Week 1) | >= 2,500 |
| Guest data capture rate (checkout) | >= 50% (up from 43%) |

---

## 4. Phase 3 -- Sustain (Months 1-3)

### Month 1: Optimise
- A/B test subject lines and send times across all post-stay email templates.
- Activate win-back campaign for lapsed one-time guests (see win_back_campaign.md).
- Weekly performance dashboard review with marketing and ops leads.

### Month 2: Expand
- Launch Local Explorer content for top 10 properties.
- Activate SMS/WhatsApp channel for opted-in guests.
- Begin segment-specific push notification sequences (see push_notifications.md).
- Run first surprise-and-delight triggers for Voyager/Ambassador tier guests.

### Month 3: Prove
- Publish first quarterly ROI report: incremental rebookings, direct booking shift, OTA commission savings.
- Expand to full 45-property rollout for any features still in pilot.
- Share guest testimonials and success stories internally to sustain staff momentum.

### Success Criteria (End of Month 3)
| Metric | Baseline | Target |
|--------|----------|--------|
| Repeat booking rate | 12.1% | 16% |
| Loyalty enrolment | 11.3% | 20% |
| Post-stay email open rate | 18.4% | 30% |
| Direct booking share | 27% | 32% |
| Guest data capture rate | 43% | 58% |
